who am I?

I’m a passionate person who cares about connectedness, whether it’s between people or the past and the present. I spent a significant portion of my previous career as a music therapist, evaluating the clients’ needs and settings goals to help them connect with their inner selves or with the people around them. As a software engineer, I continue to find the connectedness between components to components, from front to back end, and the people I work with. So building a relationship comes very naturally to me, and with persistence, I can always find the right person to talk to when I want to know more about something while staying resourceful to others who seek help. I look forward to becoming a part of a team of people who value people-culture, quality over quantity, and most of all the belief in persistence.
